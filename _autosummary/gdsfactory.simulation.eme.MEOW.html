Computes multimode 2-port S-parameters for a gdsfactory component.

assumes port 1 is at the left boundary and port 2 at the right boundary.

Note coordinate systems:

gdsfactory uses x,y in the plane to represent components, with the layerstack existing in z meow uses x,y to represent a cross-section, with propagation in the z-direction hence we have [x,y,z] <–> [y,z,x] for gdsfactory <–> meow

Parameters

  • component (Component) – gdsfactory component.

  • layerstack – gdsfactory layerstack.

  • wavelength (float) – wavelength in microns (for FDE, and for material properties).

  • temperature (float) – temperature in C (for material properties). Unused now.

  • num_modes (int) – number of modes to compute for the eigenmode expansion.

  • cell_length (float) – in un.

  • spacing_x (float) – at beginning and end of the simulation region.

  • center_x (float) – in um.

  • resolution_x (int) – pixels in horizontal region.

  • spacing_y (float) – at the beginning and end of simulation region.

  • center_y (float) – in um.

  • resolution_y (int) – pixels in vertical direction.

  • material_to_color (Dict[str, Tuple[float, float, float]]) – dict of materials colors for struct plot

  • dirpath (Union[str, Path, None]) – directory to store Sparameters.

  • filepath (Union[str, Path, None]) – to store pandas Dataframe with Sparameters in npz format. Defaults to dirpath/component_.npz.

  • overwrite (bool) – overwrites stored Sparameter npz results.

Returns

S-parameters in form o1@0,o2@0 at wavelength.

None
